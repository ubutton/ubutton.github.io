About Ubutton

Ubutton is a button which `Uber` customer to you.

- Build an Ubutton and deploy it to your site within minutes. Copy-and-paste means no coding.
- Provide real time price and time elstmate. One-click to open Uber app so your site provide a seamless expericence.
- Support all platform of mobile device, even if it is Windows Phone.

** To use the services, first go to https://ubutton.github.io, put in your address and press `Generate`. Copy and paste the code onto your site. Finally, your customers click on it and get to your busiiness!
